## 🔬 Research

<div align="center">

### Domain-Adaptive Transformer for Data-Efficient Glioma Segmentation in Sub-Saharan MRI

[![arxiv](https://img.shields.io/badge/arXiv-2511.02928-f59e0b?style=for-the-badge&logo=arxiv&logoColor=white&labelColor=0a0a0a)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.02928)
[![WiML](https://img.shields.io/badge/WiML-NeurIPS_2025-f59e0b?style=for-the-badge&labelColor=0a0a0a)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.02928)

<p align="left">
<strong>Authors:</strong> Ilerioluwakiiye Abolade, <strong>Aniekan Udo</strong>, Augustine Ojo, Abdulbasit Oyetunji, Hammed Ajigbotosho, Aondana Iorumbur, Confidence Raymond, Maruf Adewole
</p>

<p align="left">
Accepted at <strong>WiML Workshop, NeurIPS 2025</strong>. We propose <strong>SegFormer3D-plus</strong>, a radiomics-guided transformer architecture for robust glioma segmentation under domain variability in Sub-Saharan Africa. Our method combines histogram matching for intensity harmonization, radiomic feature extraction with PCA-reduced k-means, a dual-pathway encoder with frequency-aware feature extraction, and composite Dice-Cross-Entropy loss for boundary refinement.
</p>

</div>
